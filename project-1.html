Project Overview

This is a final year project. So when user performs the workout there is a chance that the performs it wrong so we tackle that problem by assist him/her through our system.

So basically our project has two parts. Posture estimation and posture correction. Our system detect the presence of human and plots the basic key points than after plotting it detects the exercise and evalute it after evaluating it provide a suitable feedback so that user correct his exercise. We covered two exercises i.e bicep curl and shoulder press but our flexible enough to integrate more exercises.
